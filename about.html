About Us

Welcome to The Spot, where the vibe is real and the energy is devoted.

Our Mission

The Spot is a community hub nestled in the heart of downtown Nashua, New Hampshire. Since our inception, we've strived to create a space where people can gather, unwind, and connect.

By day, enjoy the cozy coffeehouse vibe with our selection of freshly brewed coffees from Anchorhead Coffee out of Seattle, WA. By night, The Spot transforms into an exciting showcase of local artistic talent, from live music to comedy to karaoke. There's something for everyone, day or night.

Our mission is to support and celebrate our local community, bringing people together through kava, conversation, and connection. We create a welcoming, sober atmosphere, where people can enjoy their first or second chances.

Our Team

Michael McDowell

Owner & Founder

Mike is a DJ, entrepreneur, and someone who believes in the power of music, community, and second chances. He has been sober for over 18 years and spinning records for over 25, using music as a way to heal, connect, and uplift. To Mike, music isn't just sound, it is medicine.

His vision of The Spot was to create a space that brings people together, a place for artists, musicians, and free spirits to share their gifts, and for everyone who walks through the door to feel seen, welcomed, and inspired. He did not open a bar, he started a movement. The Spot is a platform where guests can celebrate art, love, and connection, and local talent can express themselves and grow.

Marissa Faltings

General Manager

Mars is a professional violinist, private violin and viola teacher, and trained luthier with a lifelong passion for music, craftsmanship, and creativity. Her journey as an artist has taken her from concert stages to classrooms, guided by a love for expression, connection, and artistry.

After taking a personal step back from alcohol, Mars discovered the world of kava in a Long Island kava bar. She quickly fell in love—not just with the calming benefits, but with the culture of community, wellness, and belonging it offered. She relocated to Nashua and joined Mike on this adventure. She brings her love of hospitality and her passion for creating warm, joyful, and healthy experiences for every guest.
